Drivers and control devices, by ecosystem

Every driver converts AC to regulated DC for your LEDs; every control device gives you a way to talk to it — a gateway, a dimmer, a button, a sensor.

DALI (Digital Addressable Lighting Interface) is a smart LED power supply that integrates power conversion with advanced digital control, enabling efficient and flexible lighting management.

Core concept — converts AC power into regulated DC for LEDs while supporting two-way digital communication with control systems, allowing precise, intelligent operation.

Operation — receives AC input → converts to stable DC output → processes DALI control signals → adjusts lighting (on/off, dimming, scenes).

  • Individual addressingUp to 64 addresses per DALI line, each with a unique address
  • Smooth dimmingFlicker-free dimming from 0–100%
  • Two-way communicationReal-time status, diagnostics and energy feedback
  • Scene & group controlFlexible lighting scenes and synchronized group operation

A Zigbee driver is a smart LED power supply with built-in wireless communication, enabling remote lighting control within smart-home systems without additional control wiring.

Core concept — converts AC power into regulated DC for LED operation while receiving wireless Zigbee commands to control on/off, dimming, and lighting scenes.

Why it matters — simplifies installation and offers greater flexibility than wired systems, while enabling full control via mobile apps and voice assistants.

  • Wireless controlNo extra control wiring required
  • Mesh networkingHigh reliability and extended coverage
  • App controlManage lighting via smartphone
  • High compatibilityWorks with major smart home systems via gateways

A Matter driver is a smart controller designed to power and control LED loads while supporting the Matter protocol, ensuring seamless compatibility across smart-home ecosystems without platform limitations.

Core concept — provides the required output for LED loads while receiving control commands via Matter (typically Wi-Fi or Thread) to manage on/off, dimming and scenes.

Why it matters — a unified experience — control lighting from any Matter-compatible app or platform, regardless of brand.

  • Universal compatibilityWorks with Apple, Google, Amazon ecosystems
  • Simplified setupNo need for multiple platforms
  • Secure & reliableEncrypted, stable communication
  • Automation readySupports scenes and scheduling
